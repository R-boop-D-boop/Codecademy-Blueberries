Page element style types

Headings like this are often used for titles. They use the < h1 > element and are usually bolder, or underlined, with a font weight of "900" as the boldest measure, and "Thin 100" as the less impactful.

Headings like this as often used for sub-headings, and are commonly used underneath the main heading. They use the < h2 > element, and are less bold, but can still be underlined

This is the element used for regular text on a webpage. It uses the < p > element, and can also be bold or italic
